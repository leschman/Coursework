Background Information

Adubla Doshend founded Welcome Home in 2003 when her neighbours were evicted from their home due to the downfall in the economy. She believes that all people should have homes for their families regardless of economic and social classes. Her mission, and now Welcome Home's mission, is to pair families with their new homes.



What We Do

Welcome Home's mission is to find families who are in need of a home and to work with the community, volunteers and partners to provide them with their new home. We work with many individuals and businesses to make this happen.
