Opportunities

Welcome Home is always looking for volunteers in our communities and local businesses. We would welcome your expertise (or even just a helping hand) in our local builing projects. Welcome Home does not require that you have "building skills;" we will help you with any training you might need.
If you or someone you know are in need of a home, please contact us as we are always looking for more families who can benefit from our outreach.
